What We Do

Teach

During each hour-long workshop at school, we cover a specific topic. They take place in Ms. Clark's room, D-113, biweekly. Check out our agenda or our Facebook page to see when and what we will be covering!

Brainstorm

We are constantly thinking about app ideas. After each workshop, we allocate some time for members to present their ideas and for us to look at ideas submitted from others in the community.

Innovate

Our products are never finished, even after we publish them. The App Store is evolving, so we need to make our apps unique in some way to get noticed!

Appify

This is our favorite part. Collectively, we have published dozens of applications with thousands of consumers.
